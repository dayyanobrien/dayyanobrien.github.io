Dayyán O'Brien

I am a PhD candidate at the CDT in Designing Responsible NLP at the University of Edinburgh, supported by G-Research NextGen. Under the supervision of Emily Allaway, my research focuses on improving the compositionality of large language models in conversation.

I will be in Suzhou, China for EMNLP 2025. Please feel free to reach out if you would like to meet!
