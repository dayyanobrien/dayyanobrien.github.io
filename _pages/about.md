Hi there, I'm Dayyán 👋 I'm a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh in the UKRI AI Centre for Doctoral Training (CDT) for Responsible and Trustworthy in-the-world NLP, supervised by Dr. Emily Allaway. My research focuses on improving the compositionality of language models.

I graduated from the University of Edinburgh with a Master of Informatics (First Class Honours) and was awarded the Outstanding Honours Project in both 2024 and 2023. I'm currently funded by the prestigious G-Research PhD Scholarship for outstanding academic merit.

My research interests span multilingual NLP, numerical reasoning, and developing more robust language models. I've contributed to several open-source projects including EMMA-500 (an LLM for 500+ languages) and the HPLT document-level parallel corpus.

You can find more details about my work in my [publications](/publications/), view my full [CV](/cv/), or learn about my [teaching experience](/teaching/).
